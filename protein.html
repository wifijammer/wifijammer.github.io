Lets dive into the most important part of any burrito... the protein! Which includes the most magical and delicious meats that get stuffed into the pocket of goodness which is the burrito! And don't forget the beans!
 
Beef is a very important staple to the burrito. You can use either ground or shredded beef, depending on your preference. This beef should  be seasoned to your liking, most likely with chili powder, onion powder, or garlic powder. Or all three if you are feeling adventurous!

 

Carnitas are a delicious pulled pork option for your burrito. You may have heard option from Chipotle! Carnitas are a delicious way to change up your burrito, and the flavor is surprisingly much different than your classic beef burrito!

 

Chicken is a healthier alternative to the other meat options! So this would be a good option for those wanting a lighter burrito! It is also a delicious meat!
